What We've Done

  • Education

    For the past five years, every autumn, we've teamed up with local farmers for an epic farm adventure. Picture fields of veggies and fruit trees, helping kids grasp that food doesn't just fall from the sky—it's grown with care and hard work. During these visits, we've also had a blast decoding food labels and chatting about nutrition. Plus, there were a few "Aha!" moments that turned them into little health detectives in the grocery aisles.

    Read More

  • Community

    Every month, through the gracious support of our sponsors and volunteers, we host community cooking workshops. Picture a room buzzing with laughter and the smell of something delicious as kids, parents, and our rockstar volunteers all gather for a crash course in kitchen wizardry. Starting from the basics—learning how to wield a knife without any Harry Potter spells—we move on to creating taste explosions. It's all about laughter, learning, and a sprinkle of kitchen magic.

    Read More

  • Innovation

    The real favorite is our annual cook-athon and mashup! Teams of young chefs eagerly grab mystery baskets filled with surprise ingredients. It's like their own little cooking show, an opportunity to showcase all that they've learned. Parents cheer with delight as these mini-MasterChefs whip up dishes that not only tantalize taste buds but also showcase their ingenious and imaginative culinary creations.
