What We've Done

  • Education

    "Last autumn, we teamed up with local farmers for an epic farm adventure. Picture fields of veggies and fruit trees, helping kids grasp that food doesn't just fall from the sky — it's grown with care and hard work. We also had a blast decoding food labels and chatting about nutrition. Plus, there were a few 'Aha!' moments that turned them into little health detectives in the grocery aisles."
    - Jennifer (Program Coordinator)

  • Community

    "Every month, through the gracious support of sponsors and volunteers, KidsRunTheKitchen runs a community cooking workshop. Picture a room buzzing with laughter and the aroma of something delicious as kids and parents all gather for a crash course in kitchen wizardry. We start from the basics — learning how to wield a knife without any Harry Potter spells — and the kids have a lot of autonomy at every step. There's a lot of laughter, learning, and a sprinkle of kitchen magic."
    - Laura (Parent, Volunteer)

  • Innovation

    "My favorite is the annual cook-athon! We form teams and grab mystery baskets filled with surprise ingredients. It's like our own little cooking show, and we do actually get to apply all the skills we've practiced. Not to brag, but I was very happy with my team's culinary creation — we named it the 'Galactic Gobble'!"
    - Noah (Participant, age 13)
